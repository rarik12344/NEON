How It Works

1. Buy Tickets: Each ticket costs $1 in ETH. The more tickets you buy, the higher your chances to win!

2. Daily Draw: Every day at 16:00 UTC, a winner is selected randomly.

3. Win Prizes: The winner receives 80% of the prize pool (20% platform fee).

4. Earn Points: For each ticket you buy, you earn 10 Neon Points that can be used for future benefits.

5. Refer Friends: Earn 100 bonus points for each friend who buys at least 1 ticket using your referral link!

Fairness Guaranteed

The winner is selected using a provably fair random number generated from blockchain data.
